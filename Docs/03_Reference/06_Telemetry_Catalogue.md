# Cross-System Telemetry Catalogue

**Product:** CommandShellOS for Space Engineers  
**Document type:** Reference  
**Package version:** 0.1.0  
**Status:** Build-candidate  
**Prepared:** 2026-06-29

---

| Area | Primary dashboard telemetry |
| --- | --- |
| Command | Mission phase and active objectives; Shipwide alert condition; Department readiness and limiting deficiencies; Navigation destination and ETA; Primary tactical contacts and rules of engagement; Hull, power, propulsion, atmosphere, crew, and ammunition summary; Pending authorisations and critical alarms |
| Operations | Department workload and readiness; Active tasks and blocked work; Resource bottlenecks; Upcoming mission events; Cross-department conflicts; Unstaffed stations; Automation and procedure queue |
| Engineering | Generation, demand, reserve, and endurance; Reactor and battery state; Propulsion availability and fuel feed; Hydrogen and oxygen storage; Jump-drive readiness; Conveyor health; Thermal, shield, structural, and fault state |
| Helm | Velocity, acceleration, and attitude; Gravity and altitude; Current course; Available thrust and gyro authority; Braking distance and collision warnings; Autopilot, dampener, formation, and docking state |
| Navigation | Current position and confidence; Destination, route, and ETA; Nearby bodies and hazards; Jump range and arrival uncertainty; Rendezvous and approach state; Navigation-sensor health |
| Tactical | Tactical plot and geometry; Threat-ranked contacts; Selected target and confidence; Own combat readiness; Weapon options; Defensive posture; Rules of engagement |
| Weapons | Global armed/safe state; Battery readiness; Selected target and authority; Ammunition and reload; Firing solution and obstruction; Point-defence coverage; Faults and lockouts |
| Sensors | Sensor coverage and health; Contact count and confidence; Selected track and detections; Own emissions and signature; Active search tasks; Unresolved and stale contacts |
| Security | Security condition and lockdown; Personnel aboard; Access violations; Door, camera, turret, armory, and brig status; Teams and incidents; Remote-access alerts |
| Communications | Active channels; Antenna and relay health; Priority incoming traffic; Encryption/authentication; Datalink peers and latency; Distress traffic and emission posture |
| Damage Control | Hull and functional integrity; Breaches and depressurised compartments; Critical equipment casualties; Isolation and emergency routes; Repair teams and materials; Survival estimate |
| Flight Operations | Craft roster and readiness; Hangar condition; Launch/recovery queue; Active sorties and traffic; Fuel, ammunition, repair, and pilot readiness; Rescue craft and deck safety |
| Logistics and Production | Cargo capacity and distribution; Critical shortages and protected reserves; Fuel, ammunition, components, ore, and ingot endurance; Refinery and assembler state; Production queue; Loading and resupply operations |
| Life Support and Habitation | Oxygen generation/storage/endurance; Pressurised compartments and leaks; Ventilation and airlocks; Gravity and lighting; Emergency shelters; Optional water, food, temperature, and waste |
| Medical | Crew status and casualties; Triage priority and locations; Medical room availability; Treatment and evacuation queue; Supplies and exposure; Quarantine state |
| Science and Survey | Active surveys and coverage; Detected resources and confidence; Environmental data; Anomalies; Sensor availability; Mission science objectives |
| Computer, Automation and Drones | Core and failover health; Network nodes and consoles; Active automation and jobs; Drone missions and links; Data storage; Performance and security alerts |
| External Systems and Docking | Port and connector availability; Connected vessels and transfer links; Airlocks, gear, ramps, mechanisms; Lights, cameras, antennas, beacons; EVA activity; Towing and umbilicals |
| Fleet and Strategic Operations | Fleet roster and readiness; Task groups and formation; Strategic objectives; Shared contacts/routes/comms; Supply and reinforcements; Territory, bases, losses, and events |
| Administration and Configuration | Configuration validation; Equipment discovery and unmapped items; Roles, stations, and permissions; Page/module/integration versions; Persistence, backup, migration; Safe mode and errors |

All telemetry includes vessel, source, revision, timestamp, quality, schema, and confidence/uncertainty where relevant. Unsupported simulations are omitted or marked unsupported; values are never fabricated.
